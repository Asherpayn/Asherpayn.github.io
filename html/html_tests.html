Portal 1 and Portal 2 are two of the most critically acclaimed video games of all time.

Portal 1, released in 2007, introduced players to the mind-bending world of Aperture Science, where they guided the protagonist Chell through a series of challenging puzzles using a portal gun.

Portal 2, released in 2011, expanded on the original's success, adding new gameplay mechanics, a co-op mode, and a darkly comedic narrative that explored the crumbling Aperture Science facility.

Both games are praised for their innovative gameplay, clever writing, and memorable characters, making them timeless classics in the gaming world.
